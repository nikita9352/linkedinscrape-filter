This app is for scraping jobs from Linkedin.com and filtering them by location, job title, and company name.

Hiring status stands for actively recruiting companies, make it true to see actively recruiting companies
        
        By checking easy apply you can filter table with only ads with easy apply option,

Also user can filter number of applicants by the slider on the side bar

        Language bar shows what is the ad language not desired language for the position be aware!

Workplace tells you if job is remote, hybrid or on-site

        Please Do not forget to hover on help signs around the attributes of the page!!
